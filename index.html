Food & Beverage · Meta Ads · Full funnel

How I grew a D2C food brand's revenue by 55% in under 3 months — while holding ROAS above 3x

₹2.5L/mo ad spend · 1–3 months · Full-funnel Meta Ads
The situation
The brand had an existing Meta Ads presence but no structured funnel. Cold and warm audiences were lumped together, creatives weren't segmented by intent, and there was no retention layer — every sale depended entirely on new paid acquisition.
What I did
1
Rebuilt the full funnel. Separated cold prospecting (TOF), warm retargeting (MOF), and past purchaser campaigns (BOF) into distinct ad sets with tailored messaging at each stage.
2
Restructured creative strategy. Matched ad creatives to funnel stage — awareness hooks for cold audiences, testimonials for warm, and loyalty messaging for repeat buyers.
3
Added a retention layer. Launched email and WhatsApp remarketing flows — abandoned cart, post-purchase upsell, and re-engagement sequences to drive repeat purchases without extra ad spend.
4
Scaled spend strategically. Used a test-and-learn budget framework — small tests on new audiences and creatives, then scaling winners while cutting underperformers weekly.

Health & Wellness · Meta Ads · Lead generation

How I scaled a Health & Wellness brand's ad spend 2.4x — while cutting cost per lead by 26%

₹4.5L → ₹10.8L/mo ad spend · 6 months · Meta Ads
The situation
The brand was generating leads but at an unsustainable cost. Campaigns lacked structure, tracking was unreliable, and creative testing had no framework — making it impossible to know what was working before spending more.
What I did
1
Fixed tracking from the ground up. Implemented proper pixel events, Conversion API, and UTM tracking so every lead source was accurately attributed before touching budgets.
2
Built a creative testing framework. Set up structured A/B tests across hooks, formats, and audiences — isolating variables so we could identify winners quickly and scale with confidence.
3
Improved lead quality, not just volume. Refined audience targeting and landing page alignment to attract leads that actually converted downstream — not just clicks that padded the numbers.
4
Scaled spend 2.4x over 6 months. Grew monthly ad spend from ₹4.5L to ₹10.8L in a controlled, data-driven way — never scaling faster than the creative pipeline and conversion data supported.
