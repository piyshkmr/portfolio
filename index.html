D2C Food & Beverage

How I grew a D2C food & beverage brand's revenue by 44% in under 3 months while maintaining 3.4+ ROAS

₹2.5L+/mo ad spend · 1-3 months · Meta & Google Ads
The situation
The brand was running ads, but the account lacked structure. There was no clear retargeting system, and creative testing was inconsistent and unstructured.
What I did
1
Rebuilt the campaign structure. Instead of mixing everything together, I split campaigns by funnel stage, cold audiences, retargeting, and past customers, so each group saw the right message at the right time, and we could optimize them accordingly.
2
Restructured creative strategy. Stopped random testing and switched to a systematic approach: testing one variable at a time (hook, format, CTA) across a winning audience to quickly identify what resonates best with target audiences and scale it up.
3
Added a retention layer. Launched email and WhatsApp remarketing efforts to bring back past customers to drive repeat purchases and increase customer lifetime value, leading to more sustainable growth without increasing ad spend.

Health & Wellness

How I scaled a Health & Wellness brand's ad spend 2.4x while cutting cost per lead by 26%

₹10.8L/mo ad spend · 6 months · Meta Ads
The situation
The brand was getting sales but at an unsustainable cost. existing setup lacked proper creative testing structure, the ads were not effectively reaching the right audiences, and the life of ads was too short.
What I did
1
Built a structured creative testing system. Set up a consistent testing process to iterate on high volumes of creatives, so we could quickly identify what hooks, formats, and messages resonated best with our target audiences and scale them confidently.
2
Improved lead quality through better messaging. Refined ad copy and creative messaging to better align with the target audience's pain points and current needs, which attracted higher-quality leads that were more likely to convert downstream.
3
Extended the life of winning creatives. Instead of constantly replacing ads, I kept iterating on what was already working testing new hooks, formats, and messaging variations to reduce ad fatigue and keep performance stable.
